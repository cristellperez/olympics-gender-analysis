# Growth in Women's Olympic Medals Over the Last 30 Years
## Motivation
The recent controversy surrounding the US men's hockey team stands in stark contrast to the dominance of the US women's hockey program across multiple Winter Olympics. This disconnect highlights a broader societal double standard in how women's athletic achievement is funded, covered, and celebrated. This project uses 120 years of Olympic data to investigate how women's participation and medal performance has evolved over time, which countries are leading in gender equity, and whether hosting the Olympics creates a measurable boost in women's athletic outcomes.
